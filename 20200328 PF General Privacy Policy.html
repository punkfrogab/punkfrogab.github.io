General Privacy Policy

Punkfrog AB (“Punkfrog”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This notice describes how we collect and use personal information about you if you use our applications or visit our website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation ((EU) 2016/679) (“GDPR”). We will comply with data protection law and principles, which means that your personal data will be:-

·       used lawfully, fairly and in a transparent way

·       collected only for valid purposes that we have clearly explained to you, and not used in any way that is incompatible with those purposes

·       relevant to the purposes we have communicated, and limited only to those purposes

·       accurate and kept up to date

·       kept only as long as necessary for the purposes we communicated

·       kept securely

You directly provide Punkfrog with most of the data we collect. We collect and process data when:-

·       A user voluntarily completes a customer survey or provides feedback on any of our message boards or via email

·       A user requests information or support via email

·       We make initial contact for sales prospecting

How Will We Use And Keep Your Data?

Punkfrog collects your data for the following reasons, and identified in the table below:-

·       Category 1, to allow us to perform our contract with you

·       Category 2, to enable us to comply with our legal obligations

·       Category 3, to pursue legitimate interests of our own, or those of third parties, provided your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests

How Do We Store Your Data?

Punkfrog stores your data on our Google Drive hosted by AWS. We will not transfer the personal information we collect about you outside the EU.

·       Contact details for sales are saved in the sales CRM tool Insightly

·       Contact details for customers and suppliers are saved in the financial system Visma

·       Contact details and information for support are saved in the customer care log, which is an internal system

Appropriate security measures are in place to prevent your personal information from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered, or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal information strictly to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need; they will only process your personal information on our instructions, and they are subject to a duty of confidentiality.

Procedures are in place to deal with any suspected data security breach, and will notify you and any applicable regulator of a suspected breach where we are legally required to do so.

Marketing

Punkfrog will not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing purposes.

What Are Your Data Protection Rights?

Punkfrog would like to ensure you are fully aware of your data protection rights. Under certain circumstances, you have the following rights by law:-

·       Right to access copies of your personal information (commonly known as a “data subject access request”).

·       Right to rectification of the personal information that we hold about you, which you believe to be inaccurate or incomplete.

·       Right to erasure of your personal information.

·       Right to restrict processing of your personal information.

·       Right to object to processing of your personal information.

·       Right to data portability of your personal information to another party, or directly to you.

If you wish to make any of the above requests, we have one (1) month to respond. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer in writing at dpo@punkfrog.se.

Processing a Request

We may need to request specific information from you to help us confirm your identity and ensure your right to access the information (or to exercise any of your other rights). This is another appropriate security measure to ensure that personal information is not disclosed to any person who has no right to receive it.

Cookies

Cookies are text flies placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour information. When you visit our website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

What Types Of Cookies Do We Use?

We do not intentionally use cookies on our website for functionality or advertising, nor do we share them with other sites. We conduct regular reviews to ensure that third parties have not activated or introduced cookies without our knowledge, and we will remove any that are brought to our attention.

How To Manage Your Cookies

You can set your browser to not accept cookies, and the allaboutcookies.org website explains how to remove cookies from your browser.

Privacy Policies At Other Websites

The Punkfrog website may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you navigate to another website you should check their privacy policy.

How To Contact Us

If you have any questions about Punkfrog’s privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise your data protection rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (“DPO”) in writing at dpo@punkfrog.se.

You have the right to make a complaint at any time to Datainspektionen, the Swedish supervisory authority for data protection issues.

Changes To Our Privacy Policy

Punkfrog keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on our website.
